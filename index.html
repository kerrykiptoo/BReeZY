Questions

The honest answers.

Do I need a website?+
No. Your store lives at shopbreezy.co.ke/yourstore — a clean link you share on Instagram, WhatsApp, or anywhere. If you already have a domain, connect it on Growth plan and the experience becomes fully yours.
Do my customers need to download anything?+
Nothing. They tap your link and the conversation opens in the browser. Browse, choose and pay with M-Pesa or card. No app, no account, no friction.
Where does the money go?+
Directly to your Paystack account. Breezy never touches your money. You connect your own Paystack secret key in the dashboard — every payment settles to your account instantly.
What does the free trial include?+
Everything in the Starter plan — full store, real payments, dashboard, automatic emails and review requests. 14 days, completely free. No card required to start.
How long does setup take?+
Tap Register in the widget, we call you within 24 hours, your store goes live the same day. Most sellers are up and running within a few hours of the call.
What if I have a lot of products?+
You add products through your dashboard — most sellers with under 50 products are done in under an hour. For larger catalogues, CSV bulk import is available on Pro, or we can handle it for a one-time fee.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. No contracts. Your customer list and order history are exportable anytime. If Breezy isn't working for you, you stop. Simple.

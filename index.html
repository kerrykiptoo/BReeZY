Questions

The honest answers.

For buyers

Do I need an account to buy?+
No account needed. Open a store's chat, browse, choose what you want and pay with M-Pesa or card. Your order details come to your email. That's it.
How do I pay?+
M-Pesa or card via Paystack. For M-Pesa — enter your number, get a prompt on your phone, enter your PIN. No paybill, no waiting, no screenshot needed.
How do I find a store?+
Browse all listed stores at shopbreezy.co.ke/stores — filter by category, search by name. Or enter a store code in the widget if a seller shared one with you.
What if something goes wrong with my order?+
Every order comes with a WhatsApp link for direct support. Reply to your confirmation email or tap "Had an issue" in the review email. We respond fast.

For sellers

Do I need a website?+
No. Your store lives at shopbreezy.co.ke/yourstore — a link you share anywhere. On Growth you can connect your own domain and the experience becomes fully yours.
Where does the money go?+
Directly to your Paystack account. Breezy never touches your money. Connect your Paystack key in the dashboard and every payment settles to you instantly — M-Pesa or bank.
How long does setup take?+
Register in the widget, we WhatsApp you within 24 hours, your store goes live same day. Add products to your dashboard and you're selling.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. No contracts. Your customer list and order history are exportable. If Breezy isn't working for you, you leave. Simple.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Breezy Smartshop  ·  Nairobi, Kenya

The short version: We only collect what we need to process your order and deliver it to you. We never sell your information, share it with advertisers, or use it for anything unrelated to your purchase. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and how you can request changes or deletion.

1. Who we are

Breezy Smartshop is an Instagram commerce service based in Nairobi, Kenya, operating under breezysmartshop@gmail.com. We provide automated buying experiences for Instagram sellers and their customers. References to "we", "us" or "Breezy" in this policy refer to Breezy Smartshop.

This policy applies to all customers who purchase through a Breezy-powered Instagram store, and to all sellers who use the Breezy system to operate their shop.

2. Information we collect

When you place an order through a Breezy-powered store, we collect the following:

  • Your name — used to address your confirmation emails and coordinate delivery
  • Your email address — used to send your order confirmation, shipping notification and post-delivery follow-up
  • Your phone number — shared with your rider to coordinate delivery
  • Your delivery address — used to direct your rider to the correct location
  • Your order details — product name, quantity, total amount and order reference, used to fulfil and track your purchase
  • Your payment reference — used to verify your Mpesa or card payment via Paystack

We do not collect your Mpesa PIN, card details, or any financial credentials. All payment processing is handled securely by Paystack, whose privacy policy governs that portion of your transaction.

3. How we use your information

We use the information collected exclusively to:

  • Confirm and fulfil your order
  • Send you transactional emails — order confirmation, shipping notification, and a post-delivery follow-up
  • Coordinate your delivery by sharing your phone number and address with the assigned rider
  • Resolve any issues, refunds or complaints related to your order

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to your purchase.

4. Who your information is shared with

Your information is shared only with the seller whose store you purchased from, and with the delivery rider assigned to your order. It is never sold to third parties, shared with advertisers, or disclosed to any party outside of your specific transaction.

We use the following third-party services to process your order:

  • Paystack — payment processing. Your payment data is governed by Paystack's privacy policy at paystack.com/privacy
  • Google Workspace / Gmail — email delivery for your transactional emails
  • Meta (Instagram) — the platform through which the buying conversation takes place

5. How long we keep your information

Your order information is retained for as long as necessary to fulfil the order and handle any follow-up queries. Sellers may retain customer records for their business operations. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us directly — see Section 7.

6. Your rights under Kenya's Data Protection Act

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request a copy of that information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to how your information is being used

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at breezysmartshop@gmail.com. We will respond within 21 days.

Account Safety

Will this get my Instagram account banned?

The question every seller asks — answered honestly.

The short answer: No. Breezy operates through Instagram's official business messaging infrastructure — the same system Meta built and approves for business automation. Every message the system sends is a direct response to something your customer did first. Instagram cannot flag that as spam because it isn't spam. Your account is not at risk.

Why it's safe

🔑
Built on Instagram's official API
Breezy connects to your Instagram account through Meta's official Messenger API for Business — the same infrastructure used by major brands worldwide. Meta knows your messages are automated and has explicitly approved this use. There are no third-party scripts, no browser extensions, no bots pretending to be a human clicking around your account.
💬
Every message is customer-initiated
The Breezy system never sends a message unless a customer sent one first. They DM your page, the system responds. They tap a button, the system responds. They complete a payment, the system confirms it. Every single action in the flow is triggered by the customer — the system only ever reacts. Instagram's spam definition is sending messages people didn't ask for. None of Breezy's messages qualify.
⏱️
Within Instagram's messaging rules
Instagram's API allows businesses to respond to customers within 24 hours of their last message. Every Breezy interaction happens within an active conversation the customer started. The system also respects Instagram's rate limits — a maximum of 200 automated messages per hour, which a typical store would need hundreds of simultaneous customers to reach.
🏪
Your account stays your account
Breezy does not log into your Instagram account, does not have your password, and does not perform actions on your behalf outside of the messaging API. Your account's posts, followers, profile and settings are completely unaffected. The only thing Breezy accesses is your business inbox — to respond to customers who message you.

What Instagram actually bans

Most Instagram account restrictions come from these causes — none of which apply to Breezy:

❌
Unofficial third-party tools
Chrome extensions or desktop apps that log into Instagram and simulate human clicks. These violate Instagram's Terms of Use. Breezy uses the official API.
❌
Mass unsolicited DMs
Sending cold messages to people who haven't interacted with you. Breezy only sends messages in response to customer actions — never cold.
❌
Content policy violations
Hate speech, misinformation, counterfeit products. Breezy is a commerce tool — the content of your product catalogue is your responsibility.
❌
Fake engagement
Buying likes, followers or comments. Breezy has nothing to do with engagement — it only touches your business inbox.

The technical reason it's safe

How the API connection works

When you connect your Instagram account to Breezy, you authorise the connection through Meta's official login flow — the same way you'd connect any approved business tool. Meta issues an access token that allows the system to read and reply to messages in your business inbox. That token can be revoked by you at any time from your Instagram settings under Connected Apps.

Meta monitors all API usage in real time. If a connected app were doing anything against their policies, Meta would revoke its API access — not ban your account. Your account is separate from the app's compliance status.

Think of it the same way a card payment terminal connects to Visa's network. The terminal is a separate approved piece of infrastructure — if the terminal had a problem, Visa would suspend the terminal, not cancel your bank account.

One thing that could cause a restriction

The only scenario where your account could face a temporary messaging restriction is if you personally sent a very high volume of cold DMs to people who don't follow you — from your own account, manually, before or independently of Breezy. This is a restriction on outbound cold messaging and is unrelated to the Breezy system.

If that happens, the restriction is temporary (typically 24-72 hours), it affects your ability to manually DM strangers, and it has no impact on Breezy responding to customers who message you first.
